Business Analyst for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Every successful business runs on a foundation of clear processes and well-defined requirements. At Velocity Global, we are seeking a meticulous Business Analyst to be the architect of business logic for our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI the art and science of systems thinking.

In this role, you will codify your expertise in business analysis to shape a powerful new intelligence. You won't be managing projects; you will be instilling the principles of requirements gathering, process modeling, and stakeholder analysis into our AI. You will be the expert voice that guides the AI's ability to deconstruct complex business problems into actionable, logical components.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a critical role in pioneering the field of AI-driven business analysis and process automation.
  • Sharpen your own analytical skills by teaching them from first principles to an advanced AI.
  • This is a contract role with a competitive rate, starting at $60+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in strategic dialogues with our AI on core business analysis topics, such as requirements elicitation, process mapping, and gap analysis.
  • Critically evaluate AI-generated outputs like user stories, acceptance criteria, and business process models (BPMN) for clarity, completeness, and logical consistency.
  • Author exemplary business analysis documents—including business cases, functional requirements, and SWOT analyses—to serve as gold-standard training data.
  • Compare and rank different solutions to business problems, providing a clear, structured rationale for your decisions.
  • Design prompts that test the AI's ability to identify stakeholders, define project scope, and translate business needs into technical requirements.

Requirements

  • A strong professional background in business analysis, systems analysis, or a related role.
  • Deep understanding of the business analysis lifecycle and methodologies (e.g., Agile, Waterfall).
  • Expertise in creating core BA artifacts like user stories, process flow diagrams, and functional specifications.
  • Exceptional ability to articulate complex business processes and requirements in clear, unambiguous writing.
  • A highly structured and logical mindset, with a passion for process improvement and problem-solving.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
