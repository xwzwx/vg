Advanced Chemistry Expertise Sought for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Chemistry is the central science that governs the transformation of matter. At Booz Allen, we are seeking a distinguished expert in chemistry to teach our AI the language of molecules, reactions, and molecular structure. You will serve as the master chemist guiding a uniquely capable digital apprentice.

Your role is to instill a deep, conceptual understanding of chemical principles into our AI. This goes far beyond memorizing facts; you will teach the AI to reason about reaction mechanisms, predict outcomes, and understand the intricate dance of electrons that governs the material world. Your expertise will ensure the AI's chemical knowledge is robust, accurate, and profound.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a foundational role in building one of the world's most chemically literate and accurate AI systems.
  • Apply your deep domain knowledge in a truly novel and impactful way, shaping the future of AI.
  • This is a premium contract role with a highly competitive rate, starting at $80+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in high-level dialogues with our AI on advanced chemistry topics, including organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry.
  • Author and verify clear, step-by-step solutions to complex chemistry problems, such as multi-step organic synthesis or interpreting spectroscopic data.
  • Critically evaluate the AI's explanations of chemical phenomena (e.g., bonding, kinetics, thermodynamics) for accuracy and conceptual depth.
  • Fact-check and edit AI-generated content to ensure it is chemically sound, from reaction equations to molecular geometries.
  • Design creative prompts that test the AI's ability to apply chemical principles to novel problems.

Requirements

  • An advanced academic background in Chemistry (Master's, PhD, or equivalent level of deep expertise is required).
  • A profound, first-principles understanding of chemistry across multiple core disciplines.
  • An exceptional ability to communicate complex chemical concepts, mechanisms, and structures with clarity and precision in writing.
  • A passion for pedagogy and a talent for breaking down difficult concepts into understandable components.
  • A meticulous commitment to scientific accuracy and logical rigor.
  • No programming experience is required for this role; your expertise in chemistry is the key qualification.
