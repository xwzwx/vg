Legal Expert for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

The practice of law is built upon a foundation of structured reasoning, precise language, and deep analytical rigor. At Velocity Global, we are seeking an experienced legal professional to act as a jurisprudence mentor for our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI how to "think like a lawyer."

This is a unique opportunity to apply your legal expertise to a groundbreaking new field. You will not be practicing law, but rather instilling the fundamental principles of legal analysis—from statutory interpretation to case law synthesis—into our AI. You will be the expert voice ensuring the AI's understanding of the law is nuanced, accurate, and logically sound.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a foundational role in building one of the world's most legally sophisticated AI systems.
  • Apply your deep legal knowledge in a novel and impactful way, shaping the future of AI-assisted legal technology.
  • This is a premium contract role with a highly competitive rate, starting at $80+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in sophisticated dialogues with our AI on a wide range of legal topics, such as contract law, torts, intellectual property, or constitutional law.
  • Author and verify exemplary legal analyses, memos, and arguments to serve as gold-standard training data.
  • Critically evaluate the AI's legal reasoning for accuracy, logical consistency, and correct application of legal principles and precedents.
  • Fact-check and edit AI-generated content to ensure it accurately reflects statutes, case law, and established legal doctrines.
  • Design prompts that test the AI's ability to analyze fact patterns, identify legal issues, and construct coherent arguments.

Requirements

  • A law degree (J.D., LL.B., or equivalent) is required.
  • Admission to the bar in at least one jurisdiction and good standing.
  • Deep expertise in a specific area of law, with several years of professional experience.
  • Exceptional ability to articulate complex legal concepts and arguments with precision and clarity in writing.
  • A passion for teaching and a talent for breaking down complex legal issues into a logical, structured process.
  • A meticulous commitment to accuracy and logical rigor.
