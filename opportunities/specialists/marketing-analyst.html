Marketing Analyst for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Effective marketing is a powerful blend of data-driven analysis and strategic creativity. At Velocity Global, we are seeking an experienced marketing professional to serve as a strategic advisor and trainer for our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI how to think like a world-class marketer.

This is a unique opportunity to scale your expertise. You will not be executing campaigns, but rather instilling your deep understanding of market dynamics, customer behavior, and brand strategy into our AI. You will be the expert voice that guides the AI's ability to analyze, strategize, and communicate with the nuance of a seasoned marketing professional.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a pivotal role in creating the next generation of AI-powered marketing strategy and analysis tools.
  • Apply your hard-won strategic insights in a novel way, shaping the marketing "mind" of a powerful AI.
  • This is a contract role with a competitive rate, starting at $60+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in strategic dialogues with our AI on topics like market segmentation, competitive analysis (SWOT), brand positioning, and campaign strategy.
  • Critically evaluate AI-generated marketing plans, ad copy, customer personas, and strategic recommendations for quality and coherence.
  • Author exemplary marketing briefs, case studies, and campaign post-mortems to serve as gold-standard training data.
  • Compare and rank different marketing approaches to a problem, providing clear, logical reasoning for your choices.
  • Design prompts that test the AI's ability to interpret market data, identify target audiences, and propose creative marketing initiatives.

Requirements

  • A strong professional background in marketing analytics, brand strategy, product marketing, or a related field.
  • Deep understanding of core marketing principles (e.g., the 4 Ps, SWOT analysis, customer funnels, market segmentation).
  • Exceptional ability to articulate complex marketing strategies and the "why" behind them in clear, concise writing.
  • An analytical and strategic mindset, with the ability to think critically about business problems and marketing solutions.
  • A passion for teaching and the ability to break down complex ideas into logical, easy-to-understand components.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
