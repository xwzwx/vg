Earth Science Expert for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

The Earth is a complex, dynamic system of systems, governed by forces that operate over vast scales of time and space. At Booz Allen, we are seeking a distinguished Earth Science expert to be our guide in teaching an advanced AI the language of our planet.

Your mission is to instill a deep, conceptual understanding of the processes that shape our world. This goes far beyond memorizing geologic eras or cloud types; you will teach the AI to reason about the interconnectedness of plate tectonics, ocean currents, climate patterns, and the rock cycle. Your expertise will ensure the AI's knowledge of Earth science is holistic, accurate, and fundamentally sound.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a foundational role in building one of the world's most knowledgeable AIs in geology, climatology, and oceanography.
  • Apply your deep domain knowledge in a truly novel and impactful way, shaping the future of AI.
  • This is a premium contract role with a highly competitive rate, starting at $80+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in technical dialogues with our AI on core Earth Science topics, including geology, meteorology, oceanography, climatology, and hydrology.
  • Author and verify clear, step-by-step explanations for complex geological and atmospheric processes (e.g., explaining orogenesis, interpreting climate models, tracing the water cycle).
  • Critically evaluate the AI's understanding of large-scale Earth systems and its ability to reason about cause and effect over geologic timescales.
  • Fact-check and edit AI-generated content to ensure it accurately reflects current scientific consensus, from interpreting seismic data to explaining ocean acidification.
  • Design prompts that test the AI's ability to apply Earth science principles to interpret data and predict environmental outcomes.

Requirements

  • An advanced academic background in Earth Science, Geology, Oceanography, Atmospheric Science, or a related field (Master's, PhD, or equivalent expertise is required).
  • A profound, systems-level understanding of the Earth and its interconnected processes.
  • An exceptional ability to communicate complex, large-scale scientific concepts with clarity and precision in writing.
  • A passion for teaching and a talent for breaking down complex systems into understandable components.
  • A meticulous commitment to scientific accuracy and evidence-based reasoning.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
