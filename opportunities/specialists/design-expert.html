Graphics & Visual Design Expert for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Great design is more than just aesthetics; it's a language of communication, emotion, and logic. At Booz Allen, we are looking for a seasoned visual design expert to act as an art director and mentor to our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI the fundamental principles of what makes a design truly effective and beautiful.

This role is not about using design software; it's about articulating your deep, intuitive knowledge. You will be the expert voice that guides our AI, helping it move beyond simple image generation to understand the core concepts of composition, color theory, typography, and visual hierarchy. Your expertise will instill a sense of visual taste and logic into our AI systems.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a pioneering role in shaping the visual intelligence of a next-generation creative AI.
  • Apply your years of design experience in a novel way that scales your knowledge and impacts a powerful new tool.
  • This is a contract role with a competitive rate, starting at $80+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Critically evaluate AI-generated graphics, logos, layouts, and other visual assets based on core design principles.
  • Write clear, detailed critiques and feedback, explaining why a design succeeds or fails in its objectives.
  • Craft creative and technically specific prompts designed to guide the AI toward producing high-quality visual outputs.
  • Compare and rank multiple design variations, providing a robust rationale for your choices.
  • Engage in dialogues with our AI to refine its understanding of design terminology and abstract visual concepts.

Requirements

  • A strong professional background and a compelling portfolio in graphic design, UI/UX design, branding, or a related visual field.
  • Deep, expert-level understanding of fundamental design principles (composition, typography, color theory, visual hierarchy, etc.).
  • Exceptional written communication skills, with a proven ability to articulate abstract visual and creative concepts clearly and concisely.
  • An analytical mindset and the ability to deconstruct a design into its core components and principles.
  • A passion for design theory and an interest in the intersection of human creativity and artificial intelligence.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
