Project Manager for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Successful projects are not accidents; they are the result of rigorous planning, clear communication, and disciplined execution. At Velocity Global, we are seeking an expert Project Manager to act as a master planner and mentor for our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI the art and science of project management.

This is a unique opportunity to codify and scale your professional expertise. You will not be managing a project timeline; you will be instilling the fundamental principles of project management—from Agile and Scrum to risk mitigation and stakeholder communication—into our AI. You will be the expert voice that guides the AI's ability to create logical, effective, and achievable project plans.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a critical role in developing the next generation of AI-powered project management and planning tools.
  • Sharpen your own understanding of PM principles by teaching them from first principles to an advanced AI.
  • This is a contract role with a competitive rate, starting at $60+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in strategic dialogues with our AI on core project management topics, including methodologies (Agile, Waterfall), scope management, and resource allocation.
  • Critically evaluate AI-generated project plans, Gantt charts, risk registers, and communication plans for quality, coherence, and feasibility.
  • Author exemplary project management documents—such as project charters, stakeholder analyses, and status reports—to serve as gold-standard training data.
  • Compare and rank different strategies for handling common project challenges, such as scope creep, budget overruns, or team conflicts.
  • Design prompts that test the AI's ability to create a detailed work breakdown structure, identify critical paths, and draft effective stakeholder communications.

Requirements

  • A strong professional background in project management, program management, or a related field.
  • Deep understanding of core project management methodologies and frameworks.
  • A professional certification such as PMP, PRINCE2, or Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) is a major plus.
  • Exceptional ability to articulate complex project management processes and trade-offs in clear, structured writing.
  • A highly organized, logical, and process-oriented mindset.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
