Sales Analyst for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Successful sales is a sophisticated mix of data analysis, strategic planning, and human psychology. At Velocity Global, we are looking for a seasoned sales professional to act as a coach and strategist for our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI the methodologies that drive a successful sales organization.

This unique role allows you to codify and scale your hard-won expertise. You will not be managing a pipeline or making calls; you will be instilling your deep understanding of the sales cycle, customer qualification, and value selling into our AI. You will be the expert voice that guides the AI's ability to analyze deals and craft compelling sales strategies.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a pivotal role in developing the next generation of AI-powered sales intelligence and enablement tools.
  • Apply your strategic sales acumen in a novel way, shaping the "sales brain" of a powerful AI.
  • This is a contract role with a competitive rate, starting at $60+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in strategic dialogues with our AI on topics like lead qualification (e.g., BANT, MEDDIC), pipeline management, and competitive positioning.
  • Critically evaluate AI-generated sales scripts, outreach emails, and deal strategies for effectiveness and persuasive power.
  • Author exemplary sales playbooks, ideal customer profiles (ICPs), and value propositions to serve as gold-standard training data.
  • Compare and rank different sales tactics, such as objection handling and negotiation strategies, for specific customer scenarios.
  • Design prompts that test the AI's ability to identify customer pain points, map solutions, and forecast sales outcomes.

Requirements

  • A strong professional background in sales analytics, sales operations, account management, or a related field.
  • Deep understanding of core sales methodologies and the B2B/B2C sales cycle.
  • Exceptional ability to articulate complex sales strategies and the "why" behind them in clear, concise writing.
  • An analytical and strategic mindset, with the ability to think critically about customer needs and business objectives.
  • A passion for teaching and the ability to break down the art and science of sales into a logical, structured process.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
