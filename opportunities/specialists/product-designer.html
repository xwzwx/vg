Product Designer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Great products don't just look good or feel easy to use; they solve real problems for real people in a viable way. At Booz Allen, we are looking for a seasoned Product Designer to act as a product strategist and mentor to our advanced AI models. Your mission is to teach our AI the end-to-end thinking that turns a user need into a successful product.

This role is not about using Figma or Sketch; it’s about articulating the strategic "why" behind every design decision. You will be the expert voice that guides our AI beyond simple screen generation to understand the entire product lifecycle—from user research and problem definition to interaction design, visual polish, and business strategy. Your expertise will instill a holistic, problem-solving mindset into our AI systems.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Play a pioneering role in shaping the product-level intelligence of a next-generation creative AI.
  • Apply your years of end-to-end design experience in a novel way that scales your knowledge and impacts a powerful new tool.
  • This is a contract role with a competitive rate, starting at $120+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Critically evaluate AI-generated product concepts, feature sets, user flows, and high-fidelity mockups based on core product design principles.
  • Write clear, detailed critiques and feedback, explaining why a product decision succeeds or fails, considering user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility.
  • Craft creative and strategic prompts designed to guide the AI toward producing cohesive, valuable, and viable product solutions.
  • Compare and rank multiple product strategies or design solutions, providing a robust rationale for your choices based on market fit, usability, and potential impact.
  • Engage in dialogues with our AI to refine its understanding of product terminology, strategic trade-offs, and abstract user-centered concepts.

Requirements

  • A strong professional background and a compelling portfolio in product design, UX/UI design, or a related field, showcasing end-to-end project ownership.
  • Deep, expert-level understanding of the entire product development lifecycle, from user research and strategy to interaction design and visual polish.
  • Exceptional written communication skills, with a proven ability to articulate abstract product strategies and creative concepts clearly and concisely.
  • A strategic and analytical mindset, with the ability to balance user needs, business goals, and technical constraints when evaluating a design.
  • A passion for design theory and an interest in the intersection of human-centered design and artificial intelligence.
  • No programming experience is required for this role.
