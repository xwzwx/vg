Software Engineer for AI Training (Code Quality & Debugging Focus)

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

At Booz Allen, we are at the forefront of developing high-quality artificial intelligence. We are seeking a talented Software Engineer to build the critical tools and infrastructure that empower our AI trainers and ensure the excellence of our models.

This is a unique opportunity to work at the intersection of software engineering and cutting-edge AI, where your code directly impacts the quality and capabilities of our systems. If you are a creative problem-solver passionate about building reliable software, this role is for you.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Work on unique and complex challenges in the rapidly evolving AI/ML space.
  • See your work have a direct and immediate impact on AI model performance and quality.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $80+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain internal tools and platforms used by AI trainers for data analysis, quality assessment, and debugging.
  • Write scripts and automation to streamline data processing pipelines and quality assurance checks.
  • Investigate and debug complex issues found in AI training data and model outputs, identifying root causes in code or data.
  • Collaborate closely with AI specialists and researchers to understand their needs and build effective software solutions.
  • Uphold high standards for code quality, including writing clean, maintainable code, creating documentation, and performing code reviews.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of professional experience building and maintaining robust software applications and tools.
  • Solid understanding of computer science fundamentals, including data structures, algorithms, and software design principles.
  • Proven experience in debugging complex systems and a knack for analytical problem-solving.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to work effectively in a remote, collaborative team.
  • Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
  • A genuine interest in artificial intelligence and machine learning is a strong plus.
