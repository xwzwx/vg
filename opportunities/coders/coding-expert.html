AI Coding Expert

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

The future of software development is collaborative, with AI as a partner. At Velocity Global, we are seeking an elite programmer to take on the most critical role in this new paradigm: to be the mentor that teaches our AI models the art and science of writing exceptional code.

This is not a typical software development role. Your primary responsibility is not to build applications, but to impart your deep coding knowledge to our AI. Your expertise will directly shape the AI's ability to understand, debug, optimize, and generate high-quality code, influencing the next generation of development tools for millions of engineers.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Be at the absolute forefront of AI-assisted software development and define what "good code" means for AI.
  • Deepen your own coding expertise by systematically teaching and explaining complex concepts.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Critically evaluate code generated by AI models for correctness, performance, readability, and adherence to best practices.
  • Write high-quality, exemplary code snippets and complete programs that serve as a gold standard for AI training.
  • Craft clear, concise, and technically accurate explanations for complex algorithms, design patterns, and programming concepts.
  • Debug and correct flawed code (from both human and AI sources), documenting the thought process behind the fixes.
  • Compare and rank different coding solutions to the same problem, explaining the trade-offs of each approach.
  • Engage in sophisticated, technical dialogues with AI models to test and refine their coding logic and understanding.

Requirements

  • Demonstrated, deep expertise in at least one major programming language (e.g., Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Java, C++, Go, Rust).
  • A strong grasp of computer science fundamentals: algorithms, data structures, design patterns, and software architecture.
  • The ability to articulate complex technical concepts clearly and concisely in writing.
  • An obsession with code quality, best practices, and writing clean, elegant solutions.
  • A pragmatic mindset for evaluating code, understanding that the "best" solution often depends on the context.
  • Experience across multiple programming paradigms (e.g., object-oriented, functional, procedural) is a strong plus.
