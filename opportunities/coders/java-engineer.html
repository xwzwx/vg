Java Engineer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

While AI models are often prototyped in dynamic languages, they are brought to life in the enterprise world on the robust, scalable foundation of Java. At Booz Allen, we are seeking a meticulous Java Engineer to build the industrial-strength back-end systems and data platforms that integrate our advanced AI models into our core services.

Your role is to be the architect of reliability, building the enterprise-grade infrastructure that our AI depends on. You will leverage the power of the JVM ecosystem to create highly available microservices and high-throughput data pipelines. If you are passionate about building unshakable, concurrent systems and want to solve the unique challenges of productionizing AI, this is your opportunity.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and robust, enterprise-scale software engineering.
  • Work on the critical back-end systems that deliver AI capabilities to millions of users reliably and securely.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Develop high-performance, concurrent back-end services and APIs in Java to serve and manage AI models, using frameworks like Spring Boot.
  • Integrate our AI models with existing enterprise systems, message queues (e.g., Kafka), and databases.
  • Build and maintain robust data processing pipelines, leveraging the power of the JVM ecosystem (e.g., Spark, Flink).
  • Ensure the reliability, scalability, and security of our AI infrastructure through meticulous design and comprehensive testing.
  • Collaborate with Machine Learning Engineers to create efficient and stable deployment paths for Java-based inference engines.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code that adheres to the highest standards of enterprise software development.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of professional software development experience with a strong focus on Java and its ecosystem.
  • Deep expertise with the Spring Framework (especially Spring Boot) for building microservices.
  • Strong understanding of object-oriented design, concurrency, and building distributed systems.
  • Experience building and consuming RESTful or gRPC APIs.
  • Familiarity with the big data ecosystem and technologies that run on the JVM (e.g., Kafka, Spark, Flink) is a major plus.
  • Knowledge of containerization (Docker) and build tools (Maven, Gradle).
  • A passion for writing clean, well-tested, and highly reliable code.
