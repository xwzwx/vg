Machine Learning Engineer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

At Velocity Global, we believe that the most advanced AI is a product of brilliant engineering and deep human insight. We are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer who will stand at the nexus of these two worlds, transforming raw data and expert feedback into intelligent, high-performing models.

This is not just a role for maintaining systems; it's a role for building the very process of learning. You will design, implement, and optimize the training pipelines and model architectures that are the heart of our AI. If you are driven by the challenge of turning research into reality and directly shaping the capabilities of AI, we want to hear from you.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Work at the cutting edge of applied machine learning, with a direct line of sight to model improvements.
  • Directly influence the core intelligence and behavior of our AI models through your architectural and algorithmic choices.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Design, build, and maintain scalable and efficient training and inference pipelines for large-scale machine learning models.
  • Develop and refine model evaluation metrics and frameworks to accurately measure performance and guide improvements.
  • Experiment with and implement novel algorithms, model architectures, and training techniques to advance our AI capabilities.
  • Optimize models for performance, including inference speed, memory usage, and computational cost.
  • Collaborate with data scientists, software engineers, and AI trainers to integrate new models and features into our platforms.
  • Implement MLOps best practices for versioning, monitoring, and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) of machine learning models.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of hands-on experience in a machine learning, data science, or related role.
  • Strong proficiency in Python and deep experience with common machine learning libraries (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, Hugging Face).
  • Solid understanding of machine learning fundamentals, including deep learning, NLP, and model evaluation techniques.
  • Experience with the full machine learning lifecycle, from data preprocessing and feature engineering to model training and deployment.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and their associated ML services.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with a proven ability to debug and improve complex systems.
  • A passion for turning research ideas into practical, high-impact solutions.
