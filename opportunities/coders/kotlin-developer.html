Kotlin Developer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Kotlin has redefined modern development by combining concise syntax with robust safety features. At Booz Allen, we are looking for a Kotlin expert to help our AI models master this language. You will play a crucial role in ensuring that our AI generates "idiomatic Kotlin"—code that takes full advantage of the language's functional features, null safety, and coroutines, rather than just translated Java.

In this role, you will work on a variety of challenges ranging from Android application logic to server-side development with Ktor and Spring Boot. You will generate high-quality training data, solve complex algorithmic problems, and review model outputs to teach the AI the difference between code that compiles and code that is truly excellent.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Deepen your expertise in Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) and advanced functional programming.
  • Shape the future of AI coding assistants by defining what "good" Kotlin looks like.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $120+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Write clean, concise, and idiomatic Kotlin code samples to train AI models.
  • Design complex coding challenges involving Coroutines, Flows, and Channels to test the AI's understanding of concurrency.
  • Review AI-generated code for common pitfalls (e.g., proper null handling, scope functions like let/apply/run).
  • Create "Chain of Thought" documentation explaining why a specific Kotlin approach is preferred over others.
  • Work with Kotlin Multiplatform Mobile (KMM) to demonstrate shared business logic patterns.
  • Develop unit tests using JUnit 5 and MockK to verify model accuracy.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of professional development experience with Kotlin as a primary language.
  • Deep understanding of Kotlin internals, including inline functions, reified generics, and delegation.
  • Experience with asynchronous programming using Kotlin Coroutines and Flow.
  • Familiarity with both Android development (Jetpack) AND/OR Server-side Kotlin (Spring Boot, Ktor).
  • Strong grasp of functional programming concepts (immutability, higher-order functions).
  • Ability to write clear, educational code comments and technical explanations.
  • Experience with Gradle build configurations and DSLs is a plus.
