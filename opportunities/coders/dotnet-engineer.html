.NET Engineer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

An AI model's true power is only realized when it's integrated into robust, scalable, and enterprise-grade applications. This is where the .NET ecosystem excels. At Velocity Global, we are seeking a skilled .NET Engineer to build the business-critical systems that bring our AI models to life.

Your mission is to leverage the performance and stability of .NET to productionize our AI. You will build the high-performance back-end services, APIs, and applications that deliver intelligence to our users reliably and at scale. If you are passionate about building rock-solid software and want to work at the intersection of enterprise development and AI, this role is for you.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Bridge the gap between Python-based AI research and enterprise-grade .NET application development.
  • Build the business-critical systems that deliver AI capabilities to our products and customers.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Develop high-performance, scalable back-end services and APIs in C# and ASP.NET Core to serve and interact with our AI models.
  • Integrate our AI models with existing business applications, databases, and services within the Microsoft ecosystem.
  • Ensure the reliability, security, and scalability of our AI-powered services through meticulous design and comprehensive testing.
  • Collaborate with Machine Learning Engineers to define clean API contracts and ensure seamless model integration.
  • Write clean, idiomatic C# code that adheres to the highest standards of enterprise software development.
  • Optimize application performance for low latency and high throughput.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of professional software development experience with a strong focus on C# and the .NET framework (.NET Core / .NET 5+).
  • Deep expertise with ASP.NET Core for building web APIs and microservices.
  • Strong understanding of object-oriented design principles and building distributed systems.
  • Experience building and consuming RESTful or gRPC APIs.
  • Familiarity with the Azure ecosystem (e.g., Azure Functions, App Service, Azure SQL) is a major plus.
  • Knowledge of containerization (Docker) and CI/CD practices.
  • A passion for writing clean, well-tested, and highly reliable code.
