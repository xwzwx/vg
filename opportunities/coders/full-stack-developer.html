Full Stack Developer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

At Velocity Global, we are building the next generation of artificial intelligence, and our success depends on the seamless applications that connect human expertise with machine learning models. We are looking for an innovative Full Stack Developer to build these critical platforms from the ground up.

In this role, you will own the entire development lifecycle, creating intuitive user interfaces and the powerful back-end services that drive them. You will be instrumental in bridging the gap between our world-class AI trainers and our complex AI systems, creating the tools that make our models smarter.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Gain deep, hands-on experience building products at the core of a modern AI/ML pipeline.
  • Enjoy significant autonomy and ownership over the applications you build.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and deploy full-stack web applications for AI training, data annotation, and model evaluation.
  • Create intuitive and responsive user interfaces that enable our AI specialists to work efficiently and effectively.
  • Build and maintain robust back-end services, APIs, and databases to manage large datasets and interact with our AI models.
  • Collaborate with AI researchers, product managers, and other engineers to define features and deliver high-quality software.
  • Write clean, scalable, and thoroughly tested code for both the front-end and back-end.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of professional experience in full-stack web development.
  • Proficiency in modern front-end technologies (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a framework like React, Vue, or Svelte).
  • Experience with a back-end programming language (Python preferred) and API frameworks (e.g., Flask, FastAPI, Django).
  • Strong knowledge of database design and management (both SQL and NoSQL databases).
  • Familiarity with version control (Git) and modern deployment practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently in a remote environment.
  • A strong interest in building user-centric products in the AI/ML domain.
