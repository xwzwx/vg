AI Test Automation Engineer

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

A truly intelligent AI is not just powerful; it's predictable, reliable, and safe. At Velocity Global, we are looking for an AI Test Automation Engineer to be the guardian of our model's quality, building the digital immune system that ensures our AI performs flawlessly.

This is not about testing predictable buttons and forms. It's about building sophisticated, automated frameworks to validate the complex, often non-deterministic behavior of large-scale AI models. You will design the strategies and write the code that catches regressions, identifies biases, and guarantees that our AI is trustworthy from the ground up.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Become an expert in the emerging and critical field of AI quality assurance and MLOps.
  • Your work is the final quality gate before our AI reaches users; your impact is direct and essential.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Design, build, and maintain automated testing frameworks specifically for evaluating the performance, safety, and correctness of AI models.
  • Develop automated systems for generating diverse and adversarial test cases to probe for model weaknesses and edge-case failures.
  • Create automated regression suites to detect subtle changes in model behavior between different versions.
  • Implement frameworks for performance testing, including latency, throughput, and resource usage of AI model endpoints.
  • Collaborate with Machine Learning Engineers and Researchers to integrate quality gates directly into the CI/CD and training pipelines.
  • Analyze test results, report on model quality metrics, and help diagnose the root cause of failures, whether in code or data.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of experience in a Test Automation, QA Engineering, or Software Development Engineer in Test (SDET) role.
  • Strong proficiency in a programming language, preferably Python, for writing test scripts and frameworks.
  • Experience with testing frameworks (e.g., Pytest, Selenium, Playwright) and API testing tools (e.g., Postman, REST Assured).
  • An analytical and adversarial mindset, always thinking about edge cases and how to break things in creative ways.
  • A deep interest in the unique challenges of testing non-deterministic, probabilistic AI systems.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD principles and tools (e.g., GitHub Actions, Jenkins).
  • Excellent communication skills for clearly documenting bugs and quality issues.
