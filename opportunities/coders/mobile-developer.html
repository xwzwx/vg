Mobile App Developer for AI Training

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

In today's interconnected world, users expect intelligence to be available instantly, regardless of the device they are holding. At Booz Allen, we are seeking a versatile Mobile App Developer to define how AI interacts with the mobile ecosystem. You will be instrumental in teaching AI models to understand, generate, and optimize code for both iOS and Android platforms.

Whether it is native development (Swift/Kotlin) or cross-platform frameworks (React Native/Flutter), your expertise will ensure our AI models can support the full spectrum of mobile engineering. You will solve unique challenges related to mobile-specific constraints and help build the training datasets that power the next generation of intelligent coding assistants.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Work across the mobile stack, gaining exposure to both Native and Cross-Platform technologies.
  • Play a key role in defining best practices for AI-generated mobile code.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $100+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain high-quality mobile applications and components for iOS and Android.
  • Create diverse code samples in Swift, Kotlin, React Native, and Flutter to train our AI models.
  • Evaluate AI-generated mobile code for performance, security, and adherence to Apple Human Interface Guidelines and Google Material Design.
  • Integrate RESTful APIs and WebSocket services to connect mobile front-ends with AI back-ends.
  • Write technical documentation and "Chain of Thought" reasoning to explain mobile architecture decisions to the model.
  • Debug complex issues involving device fragmentation and OS-specific limitations.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of professional experience in mobile application development.
  • Proficiency in at least one native language (Swift or Kotlin) AND one cross-platform framework (React Native or Flutter).
  • Strong understanding of the full mobile development lifecycle, from prototyping to App Store/Play Store deployment.
  • Experience with state management libraries (e.g., Redux, Provider, Bloc, or SwiftUI State).
  • Knowledge of mobile persistence (CoreData, Realm, SQLite) and offline-first architecture.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines for mobile (Fastlane, Bitrise, or GitHub Actions).
  • Excellent communication skills and an interest in AI/LLM technologies.
