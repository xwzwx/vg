AI Data Engineer

Location: Remote Type: Full-time or Part-time Contract

Every powerful AI model is built on a foundation of clean, reliable, and massive datasets. At Velocity Global, we are seeking an exceptional Data Engineer to architect, construct, and manage this critical foundation. You will build the data superhighways that fuel our entire AI development process.

This role is the starting point for all intelligence we create. You will be responsible for designing the systems that ingest, transform, and serve terabytes of data, ensuring its quality and availability for our machine learning models. If you are passionate about building robust, scalable data systems and understand that data is the lifeblood of AI, this is your opportunity to make a foundational impact.

Benefits

  • This is a full-time or part-time remote position with a flexible schedule.
  • Build the foundational data infrastructure that supports all AI model development at the company.
  • Solve complex, large-scale data engineering challenges using modern cloud and big data technologies.
  • This is a contract position with a highly competitive rate, starting at $70+ USD per hour.

Responsibilities

  • Design, build, and maintain scalable and reliable data pipelines (ETL/ELT) to collect, process, and prepare data for machine learning model training.
  • Implement robust data validation, cleaning, and quality monitoring frameworks to ensure the integrity of our training data.
  • Architect and manage data storage solutions (e.g., data lakes, data warehouses) to efficiently handle large and diverse datasets.
  • Automate data workflows and create tools to improve the efficiency and reliability of our data pipelines.
  • Collaborate closely with Machine Learning Engineers and AI trainers to understand their data requirements and deliver production-ready datasets.
  • Optimize data pipelines for performance, cost, and scalability.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of professional experience in a data engineering or related role.
  • Strong proficiency in SQL and Python for data manipulation and pipeline development.
  • Hands-on experience with modern data pipeline orchestration tools (e.g., Airflow, Dagster, Prefect).
  • Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Spark, Dask) and distributed systems.
  • Knowledge of cloud data warehousing and data lake solutions (e.g., BigQuery, Redshift, Snowflake, S3).
  • Solid understanding of data modeling, schema design, and data governance principles.
  • A meticulous, systems-thinking approach to building reliable and scalable data infrastructure.
